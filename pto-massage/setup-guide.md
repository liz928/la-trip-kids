### How to use it
SUG has a **"Bulk Add Slots"** option in the slot creation step:

1. In the SUG sign-up creation flow, get to "Slots / Schedule"
2. Click **"Bulk Add Slots"** or **"Import from CSV"**
3. Upload `massage-signupgenius-import.csv`
4. Review the auto-imported slots (114 total — 57/day × 2 days)
5. Continue to settings

Each slot title looks like:
```

```

This makes it crystal clear who should book what window.

## 💡 Why this design works

- **Priority labels** in slot titles guide staff to book during their plan period
- **Honor system** keeps the signup simple (one signup, no phasing)
- **Therapist breaks** are scheduled in the natural transition gaps between grade windows
- **Lunch periods avoided** as primary slots (teachers want to eat)
- **Buffer of 4 slots** so a few people who miss out can still get last-minute openings if no-shows happen
