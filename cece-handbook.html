🩸 Adalyn nosebleeds — quick reference:
  1. Sit her upright, lean slightly forward (NOT back)
  2. Pinch the soft part of the nose for 10 full minutes — no peeking
  3. Cold compress on neck/forehead helps
  4. After: no nose blowing, picking, or hard sneezing for a few hours

💧 Blood clotting strips on the bathroom counter in Liz's bedroom. Insert into the nostril and pinch to help with clotting — useful if pressure alone isn't stopping the bleed.

💊 Other kid meds (Tylenol, ibuprofen, allergy meds) are on the same bathroom counter in Liz's bedroom.

Call Dr. Vannaman if: bleeding hasn't stopped or slowed down after 20 minutes (if it's slowing it may take longer than 20 min, but it should be visibly getting under control by then), she's pale or dizzy, or it happened from a head injury.
